DPS AJMER


CBSE Affiliated English Medium School

DPS Ajmer Tabiji stands with the mission of overall development of students through well planned academics, Horseriding, Shooting, Skating, Swimming, Dance and Music Club (Vocal and Instrumental), Karate Club. We also have well equipped Science Labs, English language lab and Maths lab to strengthen academics. Well spread playgrounds aid basketball cricket badminton volleyball and athletics for physical development.

We believe that every child is born unique. One way of teaching does not work for every child. We need to teach the way they learn, not force them to learn the way we teach. We help children make meaning of life and develop the muscle to lead life effectively.
